Campus Involvement

  • Kappa Theta Pi, Professional Technology Organization

    Director of New Member Education

    • UNC’s first co-ed professional technology organization fostering professional, social, and technical growth.
    • Centered on networking with friendship and trust, not just resume-building.
    • Supports leadership and innovation through workshops, project teams, and community support.

  • Latinos in Tech (SHPE)

    Member

    • Latinos in Tech is a UNC chapter of the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE).
    • Dedicated to empowering and supporting Hispanic and Latinx students pursuing careers in technology and engineering.
    • Provides networking, mentorship, and professional development through events, workshops, and community outreach.

  • Tech Workshops & Hackathons

    Leader/Volunteer

    • Organized resume-building workshops to help peers strengthen their professional materials.
    • Led LeetCode practice sessions to improve technical problem-solving skills.
    • Hosted behavioral and technical interview prep sessions for KTP members.
    • Volunteered at campus hackathons, providing guidance and logistical support to participants.

Generally, in the listed extra-curriculars, I help organize workshops, support peer development, and contribute to a welcoming tech community on campus.
